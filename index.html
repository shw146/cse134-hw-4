Welcome to the Cheese Society!

Congratulations! You have been invited to join the Cheese Society, an organization devoted to the appreciation, discussion, and occasional over-analysis of fine cheeses from around the world.

Founded in 1908 by a small group of travelers who believed every memorable conversation should begin with a cheese board, the Society has grown into a community of curious tasters, amateur affineurs, and devoted snack connoisseurs.

As a new member, you'll enjoy:

  • 🧀 Monthly tastings featuring everything from familiar favorites to wonderfully mysterious wheels.
  • 📚 "Curdside Chats," where members share stories, recipes, and cheese history.
  • 🏅 The opportunity to earn honorary titles such as Keeper of the Brie, Cheddar Scholar, and Master of the Muenster.
  • 🥖 Access to our annual Cheese & Bread Banquet, where debates about crackers are considered a competitive sport.

Our Guiding Principles

  • Every cheese has a story
  • Curiosity pairs well with crackers
  • Sharing is encouraged
  • Good company makes every cheese taste better
We hope you'll find friendship, laughter, and perhaps a new favorite cheese among fellow members.
